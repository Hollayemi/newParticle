Responsible Gaming Policy

At iAuctionX we believe that gaming is a form of entertainment and committed to providing our users with a pleasant recreational experience. But for some people the gambling can get out of control and turn in to addiction. At iAuctionX we understand the risks connected to gambling. That is why we are committed to support and promote responsible gaming. We encourage our users to remember that gaming is a pleasurable pastime so they should remain conscious of decision they make on the platform, by not gaming beyond their means.

PROBLEM GAMBLING

Problem occurs when a player lack control over their gambling, particular the amount of money and time spent on gambling. The negative consequences of a gambling problem may include:

  1. The gambler suffering considerable financial losses relative to the gambler`s means.
  2. Adverse effects on the gambler`s personality and personal connections, including family and friends.

SELF ASSESSMENT

It is very important to be aware of your gambling habits in order to assess whether or not gambling is a problem for you. If you suspect that you are having a gambling problem, you can take the self-assessment test here.
